Cheese Omelette

Description

This is a Cheese Omelette recipe.

Ingredients

  • 3 large eggs
  • 1 tablespoon milk, or as needed
  • salt and freshly ground white pepper to taste
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • Â¼ cup shredded Emmentaler cheese

Steps

  1. Whisk eggs into a bowl.
  2. Add milk and season with salt and pepper, whisk until egg mixture is foamy.
  3. Melt butter in a small, nonstick skillet over medium-low heat, pour in egg mixture and swirl the skillet so the bottom is evenly covered with egg.
  4. Cook until egg starts to set.
  5. Lift edges with a spatula and tilt the skillet so uncooked egg can flow towards the bottom of the skillet to set, repeat until no visible liquid egg remains.
  6. Carefully flip omelette, cook until warmed through.
  7. Sprinkle Emmentaler cheese down the middle of omelette, fold in half, cook until cheese is melted.
  8. Slide omelette onto a plate.
