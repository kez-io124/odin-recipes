Macaroni and Cheese

Description

This is a simple Mac and Cheese recipe.

Ingredients

  • 1 (8 ounce) box elbow macaroni
  • ¼ cup butter
  • ¼ cup all-purpose flour
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • ground black pepper to taste
  • 2 cups milk
  • 2 cups shredded Cheddar cheese

Steps

  1. Boil macaroni
  2. Make a roux
  3. Add cheese and macaroni
