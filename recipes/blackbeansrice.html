Black Beans and Rice

Description

This is a recipe for Black Beans & Rice

Ingredients

  • Olive oil
  • 1 chopped onion
  • 2 cloves of garlic
  • Rice (white preferred)
  • Broth
  • 3 1/2 cups of drained black beans
  • Cumin & cayenne pepper for seasoning

Steps

  1. Cook the onion and garlic until the onion has softened.
  2. Stir in the rice, then add the broth and bring to a boil.
  3. Add the beans and spices.
